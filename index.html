Tahchin Project

Tahchin is like a rice cake in a buttery drizzled pan and cooked in the oven. When it’s ready, they invert it on a platter with a crunchy, golden brown top. Generally in Persian, they call the lowest crispy layer of the pot Tahdig. Unlike all other Persian rice recipes, Tahchin’s rice very sticky and has a cake texture instead of being all fluffy and light.
